How to add a patient?

Click on the Add a patient coloumn in the side panel or on content page
Give the details and click on submit

How to view patients?

Click on the View patient's coloumn in the side panel or on content page
Select the profile you want to view or edit.

How to add a family member?

Click on the Add family member coloumn in the side panel or on content page
Select the patient from the drop down and add family members phone number and click on add button.

How to edit profile?

Click on the edit button in the view patient's
Edit the required fields and click on update button
